Insertion Sort

                Insertion Sort works by taking one unsorted element and iteratively comparing it to the sorted 
                elements in the array, inserting it where it is larger or smaller than another element until 
                there are no more unsorted elements. 

                Worst-case time complexity: O(n^2)
