Quick Sort

 
                Quick Sort works by selecting a pivot element and splitting the array into two parts. 
                The elements smaller than the pivot are moved to the left of the pivot element while the larger elements 
                are moved to the right of the pivot element. 
                This process is repeated until the array is completely sorted.

                Worst-case time complexity: O(nlogn)
